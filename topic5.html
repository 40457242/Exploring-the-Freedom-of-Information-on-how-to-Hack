Section 5 - Online Courses... Online Courses EVERYWHERE

Tom Be

Online web courses have grown exponentially in popularity over the past years, this is down to a wider awareness in society, news of cyber related crime and a greater push for education in school to teach children from a young age about keeping safe online and maintaining a secure online presence.

Websites such as Udemy has thrived in this environment as people flock to their site by the thousands every day to learn and develop new skills. Cyber security related courses have exploded in popularity as more people want to learn the skills regarding how to exploit and hack; whether this is for personal and ethical reasons for gaining a wider knowledge or it has malicious intent to learn to exploit cyber vulnerabilities is up for debate.
